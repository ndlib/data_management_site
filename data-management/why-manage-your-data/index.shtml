Why Manage Your Research Data?

As research becomes more data intensive and collaborative, data management is now an important part of responsible scholarly communication. Good data management both benefits your own project as well as the various discipline-specific communities your are a part of (lab, academic department, university, research field). As a result, many sponsoring agencies now require data management plans.

By managing your data you will:

  • Meet grant requirements, or have a more competitive grant application.
  • Better guarantee your research data and records are accurate, complete, authentic and reliable.
  • Enhance data security and reduce risk of data loss.
  • More easily preserve your research data for the long-term
  • Increase the impact and visibility of your research having a more readily findable research data citation

Thus, we recommend that you create a strategy to organize, preserve, and share your data long after your research concludes.
